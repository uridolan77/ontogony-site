## How to read it

The book moves in four bands. Chapters 1 through 4 install the realist and thermodynamic permissions. Chapters 5 through 7 define the engine sequence that explains novelty, persistence, and stacking. Chapters 8 through 12 walk the cognitive staircase one closure at a time. Chapter 13 tests the whole apparatus against the contemporary AI scene.
