The cognitive sequence maps six local closures inside R3: Bioelectric Governor, Embodied Present, Affective Witness, Offline Mind, Metarepresentational Agent, and Symbolic Exit.

Each station has the same grammar: a coordination crisis, a mediating closure, and a characteristic failure signature.

This is not a staircase of ascent.
