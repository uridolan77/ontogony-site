## Figure

```text
new regime
   |
   +--> WITNESS
   |      redundant inscription
   |      multiple stable copies / publication surfaces
   |
   +--> CANON
          selective compression
          load-bearing variables only

WITNESS + CANON
   -> durable objectivity
   -> public readability
   -> persistence under disturbance
```

## How to read it

Genesis produces a fragile singularity. Stabilisation makes that singularity available as a reproducible object. The witness side publishes. The canon side selects. Only the pair yields a regime that can survive disturbance and become substrate for a later stratum.
