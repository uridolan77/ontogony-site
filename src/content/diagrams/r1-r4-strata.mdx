## How to read it

The registers are not stacked as a ladder of worth. They mark different binding modalities. R1 is the field condition. R2 installs metabolic closure. R3 installs integrative governance. R4 installs symbolic and political enforcement. Each outer layer folds around the ones beneath it and can ultimately reach them.
