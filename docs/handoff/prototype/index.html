A FIELD GUIDE TO THE TRANSDUCTIVE UNIVERSE

Ontogony.
A field guide to the becoming of things.

A theoretical instrument for thinking about how regimes install, persist, and fold around one another — from the metastable field through the metabolic, cognitive, and institutional strata. Persistence is priced; cessation is diagnostic; transduction is the operator that links them.
